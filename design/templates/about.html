Who we are

We are Students at ALX Software Engineering program, and this project is done as a portfolio project for the program. We are a team of 2 students who are passionate about technology and its application in solving real-world problems. We hope our project will be of great help to Speech-Language Pathologists (SLPs) in their invaluable work.


Welcome to SLP Assistant

At SLP Assistant, we stand as a beacon of support and a dedicated resource for Speech-Language Pathologists (SLPs). Recognizing the challenges faced by SLPs in their invaluable work, we set out to make their journey smoother and more efficient.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to empower Speech-Language Pathologists in their assessments and daily practice. We understand that one of the challenges SLPs encounter is the time-consuming task of searching for and navigating through the Integrated Scales of Development during assessments. That's where SLP Assistant comes in.
